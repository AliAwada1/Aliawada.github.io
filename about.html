Our Hotel's Staff Info.

Room service

Our room service staff is dedicated to providing prompt and attentive service. A discreetly placed phone in your room connects you directly to our dedicated team, available 24/7 to take your orders and address any special requests you may have. Whether you prefer breakfast in bed, a romantic candlelit dinner, or a late-night snack, we are here to make it happen

Resturant staff

Led by our experienced and skilled Executive Chef, our kitchen team is a diverse group of culinary professionals who bring creativity, passion, and expertise to every dish they prepare. They are committed to using the finest and freshest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible, to ensure that every meal is of the highest quality.

Fast Taxi

Our fast taxi staff also goes the extra mile to accommodate special requests, such as arranging transportation for larger groups, providing child car seats, or assisting guests with mobility challenges. They are trained to handle diverse situations and strive to make your travel experience as smooth and enjoyable as possible.
